    About the Division

    Erection Commissioning

    Services

    Balaji Associates delivers comprehensive erection and commissioning solutions for a wide range of industrial equipment and plants.

    With a team of experienced professionals, we ensure accurate installation, proper alignment, and seamless integration of equipment within project timelines.

    Our execution capabilities are backed by hands-on experience across multiple industries, enabling us to handle complex and multi-disciplinary projects with confidence.

    From installation to commissioning — performance from day one.
